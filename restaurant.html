Our Story

Born from a Passion for the Extraordinary

Maison Dorée was born from a simple belief: that a great meal is one of life's most profound pleasures. Chef Élise Laurent and sommelier Marc Beaumont set out to create a restaurant where every detail — from the first bite to the final glass — tells a story.

Our kitchen sources exclusively from local farms and artisan producers, changing the menu with the seasons to reflect the best of what the land offers. Every dish is a dialogue between tradition and innovation.
